Project Title: Data Cleaning in R

Description: Taking four Real Estate Sales datasets from the New York City Department of Finance Rolling Sales Data
, the objective is to plot the data, combine the dataframes & clean up the column names
in order to prepare a single dataframe for further analysis.

Data Source: https://www1.nyc.gov/site/finance/taxes/property-rolling-sales-data.page

Libraries used: 
-Readxl
-tidyverse
-magrittr
